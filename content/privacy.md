## Privacy Policy

This is a static website generated with [Hugo](https://gohugo.io/) and publically hosted <https://github.com/jsr6720/duly-noted-jsrowe>.

No user data is collected, stored, or transmitted to any server. Period.

**What this means:**

- **No cookies** - This site does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies
- **No analytics** - No Google Analytics, no tracking scripts, no visitor monitoring
- **Client-side search only** - The search functionality runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Nothing you search for is sent to any server
- **No third-party services** - No advertising networks, no social media trackers, nothing that phones home
